Meet our Cats

Meet your new furry best friend at The Tuxedo Cat. While our Resident Cats are here to stay, many of our furry friends are looking for a loving and warm home to go to. Come meet our cats and see if you meet the cat that's perfect for you!

Adoption

Adopting from us means adopting a rescue who would love a warm and loving family.

Our cats come from our partner rescue non-profit, Tuxedo Kitty Rescue. Our partnership means you can adopt on site!

Meet the Rescues

Resident Cats

Meet our Resident Cats! While our adoption kitties come and go as they find their forever homes, we have a small group of resident cats who are always here to keep you company. We're sure you'll love them just as much as we do!

Sherbet

Sherbet has been a resident since The Tuxedo Cat opened. He weclomes all our rescues and makes sure they're comfortable. He's our star!
