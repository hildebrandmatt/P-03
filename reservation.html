Make a Reservation

In order to ensure our kitties don't get overwhelmed and can move about comfortably, there is a cap on the number of visitors at any given time. Booking a reservation is the best way to guarantee your visit and avoid the disappointment of not being able to get in

If space is available, walk-ins are accepted at the front desk. Please keep in mind that capacity is still limited and can't always accomodate larger groups.
