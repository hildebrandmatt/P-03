Meet our Cats

Meet your new furry best friend at The Tuxedo Cat. While our Resident Cats are here to stay, many of our furry friends are looking for a loving and warm home to go to. Come meet our cats and see if you meet the cat that's perfect for you!

Adoption

Adopting from us means adopting a rescue who would love a warm and loving family.

Our cats come from our partner rescue non-profit, Tuxedo Kitty Rescue. Our partnership means you can adopt on site!

Resident Cats

Meet our Resident Cats! While our adoption kitties come and go as they find their forever homes, we have a small group of resident cats who are always here to keep you company. We're sure you'll love them just as much as we do!

Sherbet has been a resident since The Tuxedo Cat opened. He weclomes all our rescues and makes sure they're comfortable. He's our star!

Kesha is our second resident cat. She is inquisitive and loves getting to know our customers. Give her some ear scratches if you see her!

Little Munk is our baby. He’s still a little timid, but if he gets to know you he’s an absolute sweetheart. Be gentle with him and you’ll see!

Say hi to Beauregard on your way in! A welcoming cat at heart, she loves greeting new guests, and is very social. Maybe she’ll have something interesting to tell you!

Theodosia may look skittish, but we promise, her heart’s as big as her eyes! Give her a chance and she’ll win you over.

Sandy can usually be found gracing a windowsill, looking out at the night sky. She can apear aloof, but she’s very patient. Give her a pet!

Be prepared for Mr Irons to ambush you! Even if you’re caught unawares, if you take the time to play with him you’ll be rewarded with a delighfully playful friend.

Theodore is our other baby! Playful and curious, you’ll find him sniffing around the lounge for anything interesting. Don’t let him get a sip though!

Louis comes all the way from the French Riviera. I don’t know where that is, but it sure sounds nice! Say bonjour to our resident gentilhomme!
