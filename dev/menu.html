Drink & Snack Menu

The Tuxedo Cat bar and lounge offers a range of delightful drinks and snacks to enjoy as you relax with one of our furry companions. Choose between our cocktails, wines, and beers, paired with a cat-themed snack of your choice. Non-alcoholic beverages are always available upon request. Check with one of our attendants to see what's available.
